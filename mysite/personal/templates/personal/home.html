About

What is this..?

This program encourages students to participate in more school activities and build school spirit. This program allows teachers to reward students with good behaviour. Overall, it acts as an incentive for students to more active in the school community. Based on the scoring system, our principal will award the students with the most points Ram of the Month. This prestigious award means a commitment to fulfilling the potential of oneself and maximizing one’s contribution to the community.

Student

Want to win Ram of the Month..?

Whether or not it is participating in a school club to spark your interests and meet new people, or if it is joining a school sports team to get some exercise, this platform awards points to you for being active in the school community. Participating in such clubs and teams provides you different experiences that will help you become a leader.

Teacher

Want to reward a student for their good deeds.. ?

Beyond the classroom, teachers, you can help shape students by giving students incentives to partake in school activities using the rams rewards console. You can also discipline students by eliminating points from students when they misbehave. This console also allows for you to easily access their homeroom, student OEN number, and any additional information that may be useful in your classroom. Simply scan they QR code and you’ll be access all of this data.
